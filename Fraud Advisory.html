FRAUD ADVISORY


At Infinity Corporation , your security is our top priority. We are committed to protecting you from fraudulent activities and ensuring a safe and secure experience. Please take note of the following fraud advisory to safeguard your personal and financial information.


  1. Verification: Always verify the identity of anyone claiming to represent Infinity Corporation. Official communications will only come from our verified contact details listed on our website.

  2. Personal Information: Never share your personal, financial information with unsolicited callers, emails, or messages. Infinity Corporation will never ask for sensitive information such as passwords or PINs via phone or email.

  3. Suspicious Activity: Be cautious of unexpected requests for personal information or urgent demands for payment. If you receive any suspicious communication, contact our customer service immediately.

  4. Secure Transactions: Ensure that your transactions are conducted on secure websites (look for "https" and a padlock icon in the address bar) and avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial activities.

  5. Phishing Scams: Be aware of phishing scams where fraudsters pose as reputable companies to steal your information. Check for grammatical errors, suspicious links, and unfamiliar sender addresses in emails claiming to be from Infinity Corporation.

  6. Monitor Accounts: Regularly monitor your financial accounts for any unauthorized transactions. Report any suspicious activity to your financial institution and to us immediately.

  7. Updating Information: Keep your contact information up-to-date with Infinity Corporation to ensure that you receive important alerts and notifications.

  8. Report Fraud: If you suspect that you have been a victim of fraud or have received a suspicious communication, report it to us immediately. We will investigate and take appropriate action to protect your account.

  9. Educate Yourself: Stay informed about common fraud schemes and how to protect yourself. Visit our website for resources and tips on fraud prevention.

By following these guidelines, you can help protect yourself from fraud and ensure a secure experience with Infinity Corporation. We are here to support you and provide assistance if you have any concerns.


For further assistance or to report suspicious activity, please contact our customer service team.
